## Step-by-Step Guide to Becoming an SEO Writer
This is the most important part. Follow these exact steps to start your journey.

### Step 1: Learn Search Intent
Before you type a single word, you must understand why someone is searching. Are they looking to buy something? Are they looking for information? Your writing must match their goal.

### Step 2: Master Keyword Placement
Don't just stuff keywords everywhere. That hurts your ranking. Place your main keyword in:
*   The title (H1)
*   The first 150 words
*   At least one H2 heading
*   The URL
*   Naturally throughout the text

### Step 3: Write for Scannability
People don't read on the internet. They scan.
Keep paragraphs short. Maximum 2 to 3 sentences. Use bullet points. Use bold text to highlight key ideas.

![Scannable Content vs Wall of Text](../../assets/blog/freelancing-content-writing.svg)

### Step 4: Build a Small Portfolio
You need proof that you can write. Go to Google Docs. Write three mock articles. Optimize them for SEO. Save them as PDFs. Now you have a portfolio.

### Step 5: Pitch to Clients
Don't wait for clients to find you. Go to them. Use platforms like LinkedIn or Upwork. Send cold emails to marketing agencies. Tell them you can help their clients rank higher on Google.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
Even good writers make these SEO mistakes:

1.  **Keyword Stuffing:** Repeating the keyword so much that it sounds robotic. Google will penalize you.
2.  **Ignoring the Competition:** Always search your keyword on Google first. See what the top 3 articles look like. Yours must be better.
3.  **Fluff Content:** Adding useless words just to hit a word count. Keep it punchy and helpful.
