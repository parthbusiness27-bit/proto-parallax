<div id="what-is">
## What is SEO Writing?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) writing is the process of planning, creating, and optimizing content with the primary goal of ranking in search engines. Unlike creative writing, SEO writing requires a balance between human readability and search engine algorithms.

Businesses need this because organic traffic from Google is the most valuable lead source. If you can write a post that ranks #1 for "best coffee makers," you are worth thousands of dollars to an e-commerce brand.
</div>

<div id="guide">
## 7 Steps to Start Freelance SEO Writing
### Step 1: Master Keyword Research
Use tools like Google Keyword Planner to find what people are searching for.
### Step 2: Understand User Intent
Are people looking to buy, to learn, or to find a specific website?
### Step 3: Optimize Your Header Hierarchy (H1-H3)
Ensure your primary keyword is in the H1 and at least one H2.
### Step 4: Write Your Hook Intro
Use the Problem-Empathy-Promise formula to keep readers on the page.
### Step 5: Build a Portfolio
Write 3 high-quality samples on Medium or your own blog.
### Step 6: Set Your Rates
Don't undersell yourself. Start at $0.05/word minimum.
### Step 7: Pitch to Niche Agencies
Reach out to marketing agencies that need reliable content partners.
</div>

<div id="mistakes">
## 3 Common Mistakes New Writers Make
1. **Keyword Stuffing**: Don't repeat keywords unnaturally. Write for humans first.
2. **Ignoring Formatting**: Use bullet points and short paragraphs. People scan online.
3. **Weak Call to Action**: Every post should tell the reader what to do next.
